About Us

ToDiff is a research project conducted by the Compiler & Security Lab in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University. It is supervised by Professor Qingkai Shi.

Our lab is dedicated to exploring compiler-based approaches for strengthening software security, network security, and system security. If you are interested in our research topics or would like to collaborate, please feel free to get in touch with us.
