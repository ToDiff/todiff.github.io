    What We Do

    🔬 What We Explore

    We explore routing protocol security through both traditional formal analysis and intelligent approaches, grounded in fuzz testing and binary analysis. Our compiler-based tools — static and dynamic analysis, automated test generation — uncover vulnerabilities in real-world network systems.

    🛠️ Real-World Impact

    Our techniques have detected both general memory safety issues — such as memory leaks and buffer overruns — and, critically, silent domain-specific network breaches that cause no apparent symptoms at runtime. Dozens of confirmed fixes have landed in major open-source protocol stacks.
